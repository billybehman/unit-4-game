Crystal Collector

You will be give a random number between 19 and 120. Below the number are four crystals. Each crystal has a hidden value between 1 and 12. Every time you click on a crystal, the value of that crystal will be added to your total score. If your total score matches the number given to you then you win. If it goes over the number given without matching that number than you lose.
